The Royal Melbourne Zoo



Melbourne Zoo is one of the three great zoos in Victoria , namely Melbourne Zoo, Healesville Sanctuary and Werribee Open Range Zoo managed by Zoos Victoria. The Royal Melbourne Zoological Gardens, generally referred as the Melbourne Zoo, is an exceptional zoo that comprises of additional than 320 animal species from Australia and globally. The Melbourne Zoo stretches almost 4 kilometres from the north of Melbourne.



The Melbourne Zoo has great lavish Asian and African rainforests, Australian bushland and a great underwater experience. At the Melbourne Zoo, one can see several animals such s tigers, monkeys, Elephants, and the exceptional Australian wildlife comprising of koalas, kangaroos, wombats and emus.



There is always somewhat new to see and do every time you visit the Melbourne Zoo. Visitors can come face to face with the new magnificent African Lion bachelor group through the Lion Gorge experience. Another world class experience is the world-first exhibit that is mainly centred on creating play opportunities and nurturing an early love of nature for kids. In this exhibit the kids scramble, crawl, climb or stand look-out for example, meerkat because this exhibit is filled with animals.
