University of Melbourne



The University of Melbourne also referred to as Melbourne University, was founded in 1853 and is the second oldest university in Australia and Consequently the oldest in Victoria.



University of Melbourne is a highly reputed and world renowned Australian public study university located in Melbourne, Victoria. Founded in 1853, it is Australia's second oldest university and the oldest in Victoria. University of Melbourne is ranked 33rd by the Times Higher Education. University of Melbourne has world class programs for Education, Law, Computer Science and IT, Arts and Humanities, Dentistry and in Medicine.



University of Melbourne has it main campus situated in Parkville, with several other campuses located across Victoria. Melbourne is a sandstone university and since 1872 various residential colleges have become associated with the university.There are approximately 12 colleges positioned on the main campus of Melbourne University that offer educational, sporting and traditional platforms for Melbourne students and faculty.
