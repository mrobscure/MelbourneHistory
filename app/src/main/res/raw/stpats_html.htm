St Pauls Cathedral



St Pauls Cathedral is the most amazing and iconic landmark of the Melbourne CBD. St Pauls Cathedral is the home of the Anglican Archbishop of Australia and also serves as a church for Anglicans in Melbourne and across Victoria as well.



St Paul Cathedral is constructed on the location where the first public Christian services in Melbourne were held by Dr Alexander Thomson in 1836.



St Pauls Cathedral is built on the intersection of Swanston Street and Flinders Street making it a big part of the city life.



The Cathedral is simply outstanding and mind boggling from inside that is constructed partly Early English style planned by the illustrious English architect William Butterfield.



St Pauls Cathedral is the one place that all visitors make sure they visit due to its long tradition and accessibility.
