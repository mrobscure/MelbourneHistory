Parliament of Victoria



Parliament of Victoria is one of Australia's ancient and supreme architecturally renowned civic buildings. The Parliament of Victoria welcomes you to share in our history and heritage. When visiting the Parliament of Victoria, you can experience the political legislation and visit the chambers where Victoria's laws are completed.



The visitors can also proceed through the majesty of Queen's Hall to check out where Australia's principal Federal Parliament directed events for 26 years. The architecture of the Parliament of Victoria is simply exceptional with interior highlighting classical decoration, comprising a convoluted mosaic of Minton floor tiles, gold-leaf, columns, sculptures and portraits.



The Parliament House of Victoria is located in Melbourne CBD, at Spring Street in East Melbourne at the corners of the Melbourne CBD.
