China Town



Melbourne Chinatown is another landmark site of Melbourne that depicts the Chinese Settlements since the gold rush days of the 1850s. Chinatown is situated located within the Melbourne Central Business District centred between the end points of Little Bourke St.



Basically it runs through the corners of Swanston and Spring Streets that are intersecting with the Little Bourke Street. Melbourne's Chinatown is a vibrant, happening place and is a nucleus for the local Chinese community. Chinatown is busiest towards the Chinese New Year celebrations and similar other celebrations.
