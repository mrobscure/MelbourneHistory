Sea Life Melbourne Aquarium



Sea Life Melbourne Aquarium is home to several thousands of aquatic animals and consisting of wide range of species. Sea Life is an Experience that is not to be missed.



Sea Life Melbourne is situated in Melbourne CBD the tiers of the Yarra River alongside and beneath the Flinders Street Bridge and the King Street Bridge. One of the main attractions of the Sea World Aquarium is Australia's largest Saltwater Crocodile that is truly, remarkably amazing.



Furthermore the Sea World Aquarium contains a number of spectacular Exhibits such as the Croc Lair Exhibit, the Elephant Sharks in Bay of Rays, colours at Seahorse Pier and the majestic Gentoo penguins in Penguin Playground.SEA LIFE Melbourne Aquarium's fitting setting on the Yarra is just a small walk from many Other Melbourne attractions such as the Crown Entertainment Complex and Southgate Area.
