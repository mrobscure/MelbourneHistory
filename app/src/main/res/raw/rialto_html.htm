Rialto Towers



The Rialto Towers is a renowned Tower located on Collins Street in Melbourne. Before Eureka was released, the Rialto Towers were the highest observation point in Melbourne but is still the tallest office building in the Southern hemisphere.



Rialto towers are indeed an iconic Melbourne milestone that showcases the developing Melbourne CBD. Rialto Towers is an energetic business district for many power players in finance, law, media, investment, consulting and technology.



The Rialto Towers presented Melbourne's first high-rise building public observation deck which functioned in the middle of 1994 and 31 December 2009. Rialto Towers were also the leaders in Melbourne's first Tower running event.
